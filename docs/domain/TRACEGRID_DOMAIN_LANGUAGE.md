# TraceGrid Domain Language

TraceGrid is not a business productivity platform. TraceGrid is an information-environment collection and evidence-confidence infrastructure layer.

TraceGrid produces structured, weighted, auditable evidence products for downstream analysis by Axiom Forge and decision orchestration by StratSight. It does not produce final analytic truth.

## Why Business Terms Are Being Replaced

Inherited Paperclip terminology such as company, employee, task, report, and business objective implies a productivity/work-management platform. TraceGrid's doctrine is collection-oriented: it organizes Collection Cells, Collection Agents, Evidence Sources, Collection Jobs, Collection Directives, and Evidence Packages around defensible evidence confidence.

## Canonical Entities

- **Collection Cell** — the default TraceGrid product-facing unit that scopes Collection Agents, Collection Directives, Collection Jobs, Evidence Sources, and Evidence Packages.
- **Collection Agent** — an isolated, source-specialized collector that follows the TraceGrid methodology.
- **Collection Directive** — a collection objective received from Axiom Forge or an authorized operator.
- **Collection Job** — an assignable unit of source-specific collection work.
- **Evidence Source** — a source of potentially relevant signals.
- **Evidence Package** — a normalized evidence output record.
- **Weighted Evidence Product** — an Evidence Package or package set carrying confidence methodology context.
- **Mission Workspace** — a workspace used to support collection execution and evidence handling.
- **Collection Requirement** — the operational need that motivates collection.
- **Collection Task** — a sub-unit of collection work inside a Collection Job.
- **Evidence Confidence** — confidence derived from collected evidence and its support.
- **Methodological Confidence** — confidence derived from the completeness and quality of the TraceGrid methodology steps.
- **Collection Gap** — missing, insufficient, contradictory, or unreachable evidence required for stronger confidence.
- **Follow-on Collection Recommendation** — a request for additional collection, not a final analytic recommendation.
- **Information Environment** — the source environment being observed and collected from.
- **Area of Interest** — a topical, geographic, organizational, or temporal boundary for collection.
- **Source Type** — the class of source assigned to a Collection Agent.
- **Provider** — infrastructure that supports retrieval, extraction, enrichment, or normalization; providers do not reason.

## Accepted Synonyms

- **Collection Cell** — default TraceGrid product term.
- **Intelligence Cell** — doctrine-facing synonym when referring to downstream intelligence workflows.
- **Mission Cell** — operational/UI synonym when context is broader than collection.
- **Collection Network** — higher-level grouping if multiple cells or distributed collection structures are represented.

Collection Cell remains the default because TraceGrid is collection/evidence infrastructure, not the final intelligence-production authority.

## Doctrinal Boundary

TraceGrid collects, structures, scores, and packages evidence.

TraceGrid does not make final analytic judgments.

TraceGrid does not replace Axiom Forge.

TraceGrid does not replace StratSight.

TraceGrid may support intelligence cells, but it is not itself the full intelligence-production system.
